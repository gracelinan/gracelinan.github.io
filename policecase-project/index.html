Cooker Park Saw Biggest Rise in Police Complaint Cases

By Grace Li

Published August 1, 2019

In 2006, the New York Police Department received 108 complaint cases that took place in Cooper Park, a public housing development in Brooklyn. In 2018, the number of complaint cases almost doubled to 212.
