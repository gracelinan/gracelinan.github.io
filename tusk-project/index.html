Elephant Tusks Trade through Hong Kong

By Grace Li

Published August 12, 2019

From 1977 to 2017, Hong Kong imported 3,614 tons of elephant tusks from countries including Sudan, Japan and Central African Repulic; the city reexported 1,128 tons to Japan, China, India and some other countries, according to data from the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora ("CITES").
